about brrrilliant


brrrilliant is a consulting and software development services company geared towards startups.

We are software developers, designers and product managers that have guided businesses for over a decade to tackle new ventures and improve their IT organizations. We entered the startup space to address the lack of expertise and guidance that is available to startups.

We guide the process, assist in shaping the product, and do full-stack software development. We specialize in mobile apps and Ruby and following the efficient and risk-reducing methodologies of Agile and Lean Startup.
